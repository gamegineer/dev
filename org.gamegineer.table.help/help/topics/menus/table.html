Table menu

The Table menu commands are described below.

Name Description
Add Card Adds a new card to the selected card pile. Choose the card suit (Clubs, Diamonds, Hearts, Spades, or Special) followed by the card rank (Ace, Two, Three, Four, Five, Six, Seven, Eight, Nine, Ten, Jack, Queen, or King).
Add Card Pile Adds a new card pile to the table.
Add Deck Adds a new deck of cards to the selected card pile. Choose the type of deck to add.
Flip Card Flips the card at the top of the selected card pile.
Layout
Changes the layout of the selected card pile. The available layouts are described below.
Accordian Down
Lays out the cards one on top of the other with each card offset slightly below the one under it.
Accordian Left
Lays out the cards one on top of the other with each card offset slightly to the left of the one under it.
Accordian Right
Lays out the cards one on top of the other with each card offset slightly to the right of the one under it.
Accordian Up
Lays out the cards one on top of the other with each card offset slightly above the one under it.
Stacked
Stacks the cards one on top of the other. This is the default layout.
Remove All Card Piles Removes all card piles from the table.
Remove All Cards Removes all cards from the selected card pile.
Remove Card Removes the card at the top of the selected card pile.
Remove Card Pile Removes the selected card pile from the table.
